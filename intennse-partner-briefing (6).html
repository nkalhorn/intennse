Why This Matters

Experience the highs and lows of tennis.
But not alone.

INTENNSE is already building what no other league has: a team-first, community-first model that prioritizes player quality of life.

This partnership makes that promise structural. A shared baseline in mental fitness before the season begins. Belonging is not aspirational. It is built in.

No professional tennis organization has done this. Not the ATP. Not the WTA. INTENNSE would be the first to embed structured behavioral health into its foundation.

A differentiator that compounds. Measurable community health. Players who stay connected. A league that feels different to its core.

The Vision

A long-term architecture.
Year 1 is the beginning.

Three reinforcing channels — digital, print, in-person — so skills move from awareness to habit.

Pre-Season
Before anyone arrives
Baseline Survey

Every player and coach completes a confidential survey. What are they working on? What do they need? This customizes the course and gives INTENNSE a real picture of the community.

Digital
Compliance entry
Community Mental Health 101

Mandatory 1-hour interactive course. Stress physiology, emotional regulation, peer support, escalation literacy, culture standards. Assessment, certificate, emergency resources.

Virtual
Pre-season
Community Connection Sessions

Facilitated peer discussions that build shared language and trust before anyone steps on court.

In-Person
Move-in
Onboarding, Life Plan & Leadership

1.5-day activation. Every player walks through the Life Plan: a personal performance plan for the season and beyond. Leadership talk from someone who has competed at the highest level.

Print
Ongoing
Tangible Reference

Quick-reference cards, Life Plan workbooks, visual tools. Lockers, bags, training spaces.

Post-Season
Alumni
Continued Growth

Post-season surveys. Alumni programming. Future modules on AI literacy, career readiness, continued development. The network deepens year over year.
